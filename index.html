✕ ─ ◻ Greetings!

HASH THE BLOG

Welcome to my blog! I am Donghoon Chang, and I am studying bachelor of software engineering at Australian National University. This place will work as a journal where I keep record of what I've been doing and learning in and outside of course work. I major in Cyber Security, so most of the focus will be place on security, but I will also make posts on other things I do.


UNITY AND FIREBASE

I first started using Unity when I joined a project making an AR-based game on mobile platforms. The game re-created the users' actual surroundings, using the Google Maps API and Unity.

I worked in the team as a back-end developer and used Firebase to enable user log-in and saving of their progress in the database.

I liked how Unity lowered the entry barriers to game development and decided to develop a game of my favorite genre, zombie survival. The game can be downloaded using the link on the git repo, so check out for further updates!


CYBER SECURITY

This is the part where most of the blog's focus will be placed on. I just started my way into security, and hopefully will make posts on more advanced topic in the future.


OTHER PROEJECTS

There are also other small-sized projects that I implemented as a hobby, or after I came across with interesting ideas from online. They are mostly unrelated to each other and meant to be follow-up activities to what I learn at the university. They include web-crawling, data-parsing and algorithms.
